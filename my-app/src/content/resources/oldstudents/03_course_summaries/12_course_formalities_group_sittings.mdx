_Imaham bhante attabhavam<br/>
jivitam Bhagavato pariccajami.<br/><br/>
Imaham bhante attabhavam<br/>
jivitam Acariyassa pariccajami_.<br/><br/>

**Request for Dhamma**<br/><br/>

_Nibbanassa sacchikaranatthaya,<br/>
me bhante Anapana<br/>
[Vipassana] kammatthanaa dehi_<br/><br/>

</Box>
<Box sx={{width:"50%"}}>

**Taking Refuge**<br/><br/>

I take refuge in the Buddha;<br/>
I take refuge in the Dhamma;<br/>
I take refuge in the Sangha.<br/><br/>

**Taking the Eight Precepts**<br/><br/>

I undertake the rule of training<br/>
to abstain from killing living creatures.<br/><br/>

I undertake the rule of training<br/>
to abstain from taking what is not given.<br/><br/>

I undertake the rule of training<br/>
to abstain from incelibacy.<br/><br/>

I undertake the rule of training<br/>
to abstain from wrong speech.<br/><br/>

I undertake the rule of training<br/>
to abstain from intoxicants, which<br/>
are causes of intemperate behavior.<br/><br/>

I undertake the rule of training<br/>
to abstain from untimely eating.<br/><br/>

I undertake the rule of training<br/>
to abstain from dancing; singing; instrumental music;<br/>
attending entertainments; adorning oneself with garlands,<br/>
perfumes or cosmetics; and wearing jewelry.<br/><br/>

I undertake the rule of training<br/>
to abstain from using high or luxurious beds.<br/><br/>

**Surrender**<br/><br/>

Sir, I herewith surrender my<br/>
person and life to the Buddha.<br/><br/>

Sir, I herewith surrender my<br/>
person and life to the Teacher.<br/><br/>

**Request for Dhamma**<br/><br/>

For the sake of witnessing nibbana,<br/>
Sir, grant me the meditation object<br/>
of Anapana [Vipassana].<br/><br/>

**Closing Chantings (In Pāli)**<br/><br/>

_Anicca vata saṅkhāra<br/>
uppadavaya-dhammino.<br/>
Uppajjitva nirujjhanti,<br/>
tesam vupasamo sukho_.<br/><br/>

_Aneka-jati samsaram<br/>
sandhavissam anibbisam,<br/>
gahakarakam gavesanto<br/>
dukkha-jati-punappunam_.<br/><br/>

_Gahakaraka! Diṭṭhosi,<br/>
puna geham na kahasi.<br/>
Sabba te phasuka bhagga,<br/>
gahakutam visankhitam.<br/>
Visankhara-gatam cittam,<br/>
tanhanam khayamajjhaga_.<br/><br/>

_Sabbe saṅkhāra anicca'ti.<br/>
Yada pannaya passati,<br/>
atha nibbindati dukkhe.<br/>
Esa maggo visuddhiya._<br/><br/>

**Beginning Chantings**:<br/><br/>

Source of endless merit, of endless benefit [are]<br/>
the Buddha's vibrations of nibbana,<br/>
of Dhamma, of enlightenment!<br/><br/>

May they arise on the [top of the] head every moment,<br/>
in the heart every moment<br/>
in every particle of the body every moment!<br/><br/>

Source of endless merit, of endless benefit [are]<br/>
the Dhamma's vibrations of nibbana,<br/>
of wisdom, of enlightenment!<br/><br/>

May they arise on the [top of the] head every moment,<br/>
in the heart every moment<br/>
in every particle of the body every moment!<br/><br/>

Source of endless merit, of endless benefit [are]<br/>
the Sangha's vibrations of nibbana,<br/>
of Dhamma, of enlightenment!<br/><br/>

May they arise on the [top of the] head every moment,<br/>
in the heart every moment<br/>
in every particle of the body every moment!<br/><br/>

**Closing Chantings**<br/><br/>

Impermanent truly are saṅkhāra,<br/>
by nature constantly arising and vanishing.<br/>
When they arise and are eradicated,<br/>
their cessation brings true happiness.<br/><br/>

Through countless births in the cycle of existence<br/>
I have run, in vain<br/>
seeking the builder of this house;<br/>
and again and again I faced the suffering of new birth.<br/><br/>

Oh housebuilder! Now you are seen.<br/>
You shall not build a house aagain for me.<br/>
All your beams are broken,<br/>
the ridgepole is shattered.<br/>
The mind has become freed from conditioning;<br/>
the end of craving has been reached.<br/><br/>

"Impermanent are all compounded things."<br/>
When one perceives this with insight,<br/>
then one turns away from suffering;<br/>
this is the path of purification.<br/><br/>

**Chanting on Day Eight Evening**<br/><br/>

Wherever one directs one's attention<br/>
within the body, one is aware only of arising and falling.<br/>
One experiences ecstasy and bliss;<br/>
then one apprehends the Deathless.]<br/><br/>

May the yakkhas, devas and brahmas<br/>
of all the universes<br/>
rejoice in this wholesome deed performed by us,<br/>
which is productive of all happiness.<br/>
May they all,<br/>
unitedly devoted to the Teaching,<br/>
be without negligence,<br/>
especially in [giving] protection.<br/><br/>

We share [with them] equally the merit<br/>
of this and other wholesome deeds.<br/>
May they all accept with joy [our sharing of merit],<br/>
and may the earth stand witness [to it]!<br/><br/>
